The Best Movies According to Amy

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

When Harry Met Sally.

A romcom movie + Meg Ryan = a cult classic.



Sound of Music

This musical was my whole childhood. There was something about a singing nun turned nanny that envoked my imagination. Plus I've always been a sucker for any movie that Julie Andrews stars in.


Die Hard

Bruce Willis, what a legend. Action movies were never the same after. ''Yippee-Ki-Yay...''
